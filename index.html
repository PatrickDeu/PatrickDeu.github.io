Portfolio Sonification

About Analysis

This website presents audio examples that I created as part of my master's thesis on the sonification of portfolios.

For the generation of the exemplary sonifications, I use the global factor data by Jensen, Kelly, and Pedersen.

In addition to conventional performance metrics and the sonification of the factor portfolios, I provide a numerical proxy for the perceived annoyance based on the psychoacoustic annoyance model introduced by Zwicker and Fastl.

As the sonification creates stereo sounds, I recommend using headphones and lowering the system volume before playback, since some examples contain loud and shrill sounds.
